Automatic Fire Extinguishing System

Click for the introduction video
The Automatic Fire Extinguishing System is continuously scanned using an infrared scanner. When the pre-set temperature level is exceeded, the alarm in the Control Center is activated. It also allows remote monitoring through the built-in camera. The device automatically rotates to the fire location and sprays water or foam onto the fire using the nozzle built on it. The device can be easily mounted in any desired location with simple assembly. The Automatic Fire Extinguishing System can operate automatically from a distance and perform fire extinguishing without the need for trained personnel, ensuring rotation to the required direction and height. The Automatic Fire Extinguishing System can optionally spray water or foam.
