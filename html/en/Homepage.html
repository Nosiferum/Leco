About Us

With over 30 years of experience in the plumbing industry, Leco Engineering, the leading company in water fire extinguishing systems for highway tunnels, provides a solution partnership for all processes related to tunnels with its in-house team members. Water fire system design, mechanical calculations, selection of necessary pumps and systems, control of the entire system's health operations, preparation of approval files, procurement of all necessary materials that may be required from numerous foreign and domestic suppliers, installation of the entire system with our in-house installation team, commissioning the system, conducting all tests, and then carrying out the temporary acceptance of the systems to prevent freezing are the responsibility of our company. By solving all these processes in-house, Leco Engineering provides a solution partnership to its distinguished customers. As a conscious leader firm, Leco Engineering aims to improve itself every day and aims to follow all developments in the world and bring them to Turkey in this regard. LECO Engineering is advancing rapidly towards being a leader, elite, brave, and authoritative name that means "LECO Engineering." Focusing its vision on this, Leco Engineering, which is known and recognized both nationally and internationally, has set itself the goal of bringing different projects every day. We are progressing with determination towards achieving this with the support of our valuable customers. As LECO Engineering family, we thank our valuable customers who have supported us so far. LECO Engineering means TRUST.

OUR VISION
